FAQs

Which movies I can watch on Vimeo?

With the Festival Pass you can stream ALL the films we have in the program, from August 21 to 30, 2020 for 7 days long.

How can I buy the Festival Pass and pay?

On our Vimeo page, you can purchase the Festival Pass by clicking RENT ALL button. You can pay either via credit card or Paypal.

How do I receive the exclusive festival bag from you?

As soon as you buy the Festival Pass on our Vimeo Page, you will receive an Email from us to fill in the shipping form.

I just wanna watch one film in your program, do I have to buy the Festival Pass?

Yes, because we have only one ticket-package “Festival Pass” and the great thing is you can watch ALL the films with single pass, so call up your friends and stream together.

Can I purchase Festival pass in advance?

Of course, you can already purchase the pass from the beginning of August. As mentioned, all the films will be available for viewing from August 21 to 30, 2020.

I live outside Germany, can I buy the Festival Pass and stream online?

We are sorry, but our Festival Passes are only available within Germany. The online access of the films is also restricted only in Germany as well.

Do those films have subtitles?

All our films have English subtitles. For more information you can find on each film description on Vimeo.
