Taiwan Film Festival - TFF

Taiwan Film Festival is the largest festival in Europe that offers a platform for Taiwan’s diverse film scene. The focus of the film festival is primarily on excellent conceptual and artistic films that provide unique perspectives on the Taiwanese and Asian arts. On event days, in addition to unique film series, we present multifacetedprograms, discussions and discussions with filmmakers and experts from the Far East. A continuous dialogue process will be accompanied by numerous film screenings and creative cultural exchange. Our goal is to create an intercultural bridge between Germany and the Taiwanese arts and to promote new visions of cross-cultural understanding in terms of Taiwan and Asia.
